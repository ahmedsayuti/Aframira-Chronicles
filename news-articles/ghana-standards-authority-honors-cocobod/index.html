Ghana Standards Authority Honors COCOBOD and Dr. Joel Cox Banahene for Excellence in Quality Leadership

Ghana Standards Authority
Official Release • December 10, 2019 • 4 min read
15,234 views
Share this article:

A Publication by the Ghana Standards Authority — December 10, 2019

The Ghana Standards Authority (GSA) is proud to announce that the Ghana Cocoa Board (COCOBOD) has been awarded the prestigious Diamond Level 4 Award at the 2019 National Quality Awards (NQA). This recognition celebrates COCOBOD’s exceptional commitment to maintaining world-class quality systems, analytical excellence, and leadership in promoting Ghana’s global reputation for premium cocoa production.

COCOBOD emerged as the overall institutional winner in the Research and Quality Management Category, earning an impressive 37 out of 40 points, the highest score among 30 competing organizations across Ghana’s industrial and research sectors. This landmark achievement reflects COCOBOD’s rigorous application of international quality standards, continuous process improvement, and data-driven innovation in its operations.

In particular, Dr. Joel Cox Menka Banahene, Principal Research Officer and Head of Analytical Instrumentation at COCOBOD, was specially recognized for his outstanding scientific leadership and groundbreaking contributions to analytical method development for pesticide residue and mycotoxin testing. His work in developing and validating advanced Gas Chromatograph Negative Chemical Ionization Mass Spectrometry (GC-NCI-MS) methods and multi-mycotoxin analysis protocols has transformed COCOBOD’s quality control systems and positioned Ghana as a global leader in cocoa safety and quality assurance.

Speaking at the ceremony, Professor Alex Dodoo, Director-General of the Ghana Standards Authority, commended COCOBOD for setting “a national benchmark for quality excellence and technical innovation,” noting that the organization’s performance exemplifies the spirit of the National Quality Awards to promote excellence, consumer protection, and industrial competitiveness.

The Diamond Level 4 Award is the highest distinction conferred by the Ghana Standards Authority, recognizing organizations that consistently demonstrate exemplary quality management systems, adherence to global best practices, and measurable impact on the national economy. COCOBOD’s recognition underscores Ghana’s continuing leadership in quality assurance and its unwavering commitment to maintaining the integrity of its cocoa—the nation’s most prized export commodity.
